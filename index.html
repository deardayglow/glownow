Dayglow Cafe Restaurant and Bar is a vibrant and inviting eatery located in the heart of Bhadrapur-8, Sagarmatha Chowk in Jhapa. Our restaurant is the perfect destination for anyone looking for delicious food, great drinks and a welcoming atmosphere.

  • Delicious food: Our menu features a wide variety of dishes to suit all tastes, from classic Western cuisine to authentic Nepalese and Indian dishes, all made with the freshest ingredients and prepared to perfection.
  • Wide range of drinks: Our bar offers a wide range of drinks, from classic cocktails to local beers and wines, perfect for unwinding after a long day.
  • Relaxed and casual atmosphere: Our restaurant has a warm and inviting atmosphere, with a relaxed and casual vibe, perfect for a casual lunch with friends, a romantic dinner or a family gathering.
  • Large outdoor seating area: We have a large outdoor seating area, perfect for enjoying a meal or a drink in the beautiful Jhapa weather.
  • Excellent service: Our team of friendly and attentive staff are on hand to ensure that your experience at Dayglow Cafe is one to remember.
  • Special events: We also cater for special events such as birthday parties, corporate events and more.
  • Free Wi-Fi: Our restaurant is equipped with free Wi-Fi for our customers.
  • Takeaway service: We also offer a convenient takeaway service for those who would like to enjoy our delicious food at the comfort of their home.
  • Affordable prices: Our prices are reasonable and affordable for everyone.
  • Open 7 days a week: Dayglow Cafe Restaurant and Bar is open 7 days a week, so you can enjoy our delicious food and drinks whenever you like.

We offer a convenient home delivery service in the Bhadrapur area, so you can enjoy our delicious food at the comfort of your own home.
